This is about the game pubg. This game is muliplayer online game on phones and pc. Different opertors have different nationalities, weapons, gadgets. If you are bigginer you can play causal matches and when you will we above 30 rank, you can play rank matches. There are two Rounds. In first Round they will help to play. In 2 Round it will update.
